Preprints & Under Review
BEACON: Budget-Efficient Discovery of Policy Violations in LLMs via Cognitive-Guided Monte Carlo Tree Search
Jie Wang, et al.
IJCAI 2026 Under Review (Phase 2, All Positive Reviews)
We reframe LLM safety evaluation as budget-constrained failure discovery. Our Cognitive-Guided MCTS achieves 85.5–100% ASR across 6 frontier LLMs, discovering failures 3.7× faster than the strongest baseline.
EviGuard: Evidence-Guided Connector Intervention for Cross-Modal Safety Unlearning in Multimodal LLMs
Jie Wang, et al.
ACM MM 2026 Under Review
We identify that cross-modal risk concentrates in a low-rank subspace at the visual-language connector. EviGuard reduces over-refusal rate from 30.3% to 22.3%, achieving 9.8% ASR and 68.0% Safe & Effective rate on OOD benchmarks.

VidTouch: A Multimodal Benchmark Dataset for Dynamic Visuo-Tactile Fabric Recognition
Jie Wang, et al.
ACM MM 2026 Dataset Track, Under Review
The first dynamic multimodal benchmark for fabric recognition with 145 categories. Our X3D + EfficientNet + MLP fusion achieves 98.6% in-category accuracy, revealing zero-shot generalization as an open challenge.
